IBM DECOMPILER

An AI retro vintage IBM decompiler is a decompiler that uses artificial intelligence to decompile IBM mainframe programs. These programs are typically written in COBOL, PL/I, or Assembler. The decompiler works by first identifying the different parts of the program, such as the data structures, variables, and control flow. It then uses AI to infer the relationships between these parts and to reconstruct the original source code.
